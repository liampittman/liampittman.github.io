About Me

Professional Photo

I'm a data journalist with a Master's in Geography and five years of experience turning complex spatial data into stories that matter. My background in GIS and remote sensing gives me a unique perspective on how location shapes everything from climate policy to social justice.

I specialize in uncovering patterns that exist at the intersection of place and data, using advanced spatial analysis techniques to reveal stories that traditional journalism might miss. Whether it's tracking environmental changes through satellite imagery or analyzing demographic shifts through census data, I bring scientific rigor to investigative reporting.
