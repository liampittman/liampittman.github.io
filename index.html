About Me

Professional Photo

I'm a data journalist currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in geography and data analysis. For the past five years, I've specialized in election data analysis, uncovering patterns of voter access that have earned national recognition and multiple awards. My research has been cited in NAACP court cases addressing voting rights.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I analyzed health data to help communities understand the virus's geographic spread. I've also mapped Confederate monuments across Mississippi, exploring how history and geography intersect with social justice.

I believe data tells its most important stories when viewed through a geographic lens. Whether I'm tracking polling place changes or analyzing demographic shifts, I use mapping and spatial analysis to reveal patterns that matter to communities and hold institutions accountable.
